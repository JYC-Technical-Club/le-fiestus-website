YOUTH CLUB

JYC

What goes on

JUIT Youth Club (JYC) is the name of enthusiasm, development, consistency, and effort. It widens students' view from academics to various fields such as sports, literary, cultural, and technical and provides opportunities to explore them. It encourages students' expression, celebration, recognition, and integration while fostering qualities like teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership.

Moreover, it alleviates students from academic pressure by organizing events, fests, and other extracurricular activities. Focusing on technical, literary, sports, and cultural scopes, these competitive activities not only provide relief from intense academic load but also present an opportunity to instill confidence, encourage teamwork, and give students a strong sense of achievement and belonging.
